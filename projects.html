CS Projects

Melee Projection Tool - I wrote a basic machine learning tool that predicts which player will win a match given skill level, character used, and past encounters. Writeup and misc notes.

Input Latency Detection in Expert-Level Gamers - My Yale undergraduate senior thesis project, where I created a double-blind input latency perception task with Arduino and tested high-level gamers against a control group, and found a significant difference between them. The Thesis can be found here (although the formatting seems to have become a bit wonky), and the final paper is still being worked on.

DIY Stuff

Digital-Analog Conversion Buttons on a Gamecube Controller - I wired a bunch of tact switches onto a Gamecube controller in such a way that they would precisely return analog values. It proved extremely controversial, amassing over 200 comments on Reddit. Pictures and thoughts here

Adjustable Flickering LED - A really basic Arduino project, I made an LED flicker at a precise speed and used it to explore the idea of "Framerate" in Human Visual Perception.
