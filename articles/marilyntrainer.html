Practice Notes

  • nü is typed as nyu.
  • lü is typed as lyu.
  • Ø- and -Ø may be omitted or typed with a period. The checker ignores periods.
  • -(e)n and -(e)ng accept both forms.
  • Only single-syllable entries from wordlist.csv that map cleanly to the current encoding table are included.
  • Type the person clue and the specific location clue.
  • Full clue text works, and distinctive short forms like shinji or rope bridge are accepted.
