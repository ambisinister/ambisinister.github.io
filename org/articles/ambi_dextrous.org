#+TITLE: Ambi Dextrous

* Ambi Dextrous

[[../images/ambipack1/ambi_dextrous_bn.png]]

Ambi Dextrous is a pack of tech charts for [[https://www.itgmania.com/][ITGMania]], stepped by myself. The pack targets the 10-12 block range with difficulties spanning 8-13. Most charts have one difficulty (with one exception), and the pack is mostly streamy BXF, light on the X.

The pack is available via Google Drive [[https://drive.google.com/file/d/1EOIYQF1gRVepqDE0rNR1F7tvxbM_5UYH/view?usp=sharing][here]] (currently WIP: needs pad testing). It uses null sync. 

This page holds some information about the charts, mostly as residual space for me to keep track of all the tasks I need to complete before releasing the pack.

** Another Life (feat. Rema) [SX10]

[[../images/ambipack1/another_life_bn.png]]

*Tech Description* - BXF

*Author Score* - <<need to playtest>>

The core idea in this chart is inspired by the [[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jH_k3vU80g0][270 turns in Starstrukk [SX10]​]], attempting to do something similar but with a 270 footswitch. 

[[../images/from_clipboard/20260617_192450.png]]

The important challenge here was making the resulting pattern resolve nicely if you do this 270 footswitch or if you doublestep this last crossover and play this as a normal forward-facing footswitch. These leave you in different states (one leaves you crossed over and one doesn't) so making it all work was a nice little puzzle.

The main difficult part of this chart is the Rema verse, which is just 157 bpm techy BXF stuff -- this is what makes the chart harder than a 9 but it should all be pretty easy to read.

** Crazy (feat. PinkPanthress) [SX11]

[[../images/ambipack1/crazy_bn.png]]

**Tech Description** - HS BT FS

*Author Score* - <<need to playtest>>

I like how this chart turned out, I think the holdswitch theme fits the song really well. I was on the fence about including the "learning" notes at the start to establish the theme or not. I ultimately went with it, since everybody always draws these exact arrows when explaining how holdswitches work. Putting these right at the beginning of the chart with nothing threatening surrounding them feels like we're introducing a mechanic you will need to clear the stage later, when we need you to seamlessly transition from footswitch stream directly into this doublestep-holdswitch-bracket-tap idea.

I am a little worried this one might be a bit tough for 11 block, but I don't think it's dense or twisty enough to justify a 12 rating at just 130 bpm. Hopefully we see during playtesting.

** Fly Away [SX11]

[[../images/ambipack1/Fly_away_bn.png]]

*Tech Description* - KS BR ST

*Author Score* - <<need to playtest>>

A streamy kickswitch chart. The brackets make the chart more comfortable but you can jump them and get away with it for the most part, they aren't the core focus of the chart. It's a very streamy chart but it's pretty slow and the kickswitches break up the stream a bit. It's like 16* - 7* - 7* @ 132 with techy breaks throughout, so I think 11 is pretty appropriate. 

** Heads Will Roll (A-Trak Remix) [SX10]

[[../images/ambipack1/headswillroll_bn.png]]

*Tech Description* - No Tech, 16 measures stream

*Author Score* - <<need to playtest>>

A pretty comfy no tech chart. The core idea of the chart is the alternating triangles in the stream, and everything is constructed around making those feel fun to play. Not much else to say here since it's the pack's no tech chart -- hopefully it's the most straightforward chart in there.

** Number 1 Boy [SX8]

[[../images/ambipack1/number_1_boy_bn.png]]

*Tech Description* - DS XO SS XMOD-

*Author Score* - <<need to playtest>>

My wife asked me to step this, so I targeted a difficulty range she would enjoy. There are some cute ideas in this one, I think. I wanted it to feel like an old school ITG chart, as a part of it being funny. The bracket taps (woo!) are nicely separated from everything to evoke that they should be played as a hand like [[https://youtu.be/8d14lkaW8dY?si=OccXamJ9JiYHC2B9][Bend Your Mind [SX10]​]]. Likewise I think the XMOD gimmick in this chart accents the joke in the song.

** The Third Sanctuary [SH9] [SX13]

[[../images/ambipack1/third_sanctuary_bn.png]]

**Tech Description** - [SH] FS++ XO SS- [SX] BR FS HT 

*Author Score* - <<need to playtest>> / <<need to playtest>>

I wrote two difficulties for this because I had some difficulty briding the gap between this being a deltarune song and a breakcore remix.

The hard difficulty is an easy footswitch chart with some minor crossover elements here and there. I was tempted to leave it at 8 block, but I think compared to [[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IHnlHJuvEsE][Cry For You]] from ITL 2026 this chart is like 40 bpm faster, has way more footswitches, and also has crossovers. There's also some mine avoid elements to this chart which make it a little more punishing.

The expert difficulty is a 180 bpm breakcore BRFS romp. I have no idea how to properly rate stuff which is this hard because I don't play too much tech on pad past the 13 block at the moment. Most of my playtesting on this was done with ratemods. I could see it being a 14.

It felt appropriate for the pack to have a boss monster, so here it is.
